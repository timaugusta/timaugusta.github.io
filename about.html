How Gosling's Landed

Gosling’s Landing is a newly renovated, family run hostel tucked into a quiet neighbourhood in the center of the city. We not only aim to provide affordable accommodation for travellers, but also a home away from home. We have events such as family dinners, boardgame nights, evening socials, and organized outings to help everyone get connected.

We have a nice and big common area with comfy couches and a workspace for those looking to get in a few hours on the laptop. Our kitchen is also well equipped for travellers who like to cook. Whether you’re here for a few days or a few weeks, we’ll take you under our wing. No Gosling gets left behind!
